What are Templates?

A template is a generic model or pattern from which other objects are based or derived. Okay! I know that's all grammar so let me explain what templates are in web development.

Website templates are preset codes that produce beautiful ready-made websites. Most times this websites are made of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Don't get worried that you're not good with JavaScript. When working with templates, you might not even need to touch the JavaScript. Just like WordPress or website creation and Canva templates for graphic designs, HTML and CSS also have preset templates that are open to use.

Why use Templates?

Templates makes work easier, as long as your clients don't already have an image of what they want their websites to be, you can use templates to give them ideas and make your work faster.

Using templates doesn't make you less professional, as long as you understand the major codes behind the template. Templates also teach you arrangement and gives you more understanding of CSS.
